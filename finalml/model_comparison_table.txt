## Key Findings:

### Individual Model Performance:
- **XGBoost** achieves the highest overall performance across all metrics
- **SVM** and **GBC** show very similar performance with slight variations
- XGBoost outperforms both SVM and GBC by approximately 0.7-0.8% in accuracy

### Model Combinations:
- **SVM+GBC+XGB** (ensemble) achieves the best performance, matching XGBoost
- **SVM+GBC** and **SVM+XGB** combinations maintain SVM-level performance
- **GBC+XGB** combination shows slight improvement in precision compared to individual models

### Performance Analysis:
- All models demonstrate excellent performance (>97% accuracy)
- XGBoost shows consistent superiority in all evaluation metrics
- Ensemble methods provide marginal improvements but don't significantly exceed the best individual model
- The consistency across metrics suggests robust model performance with minimal overfitting

### Recommendations:
- **XGBoost** is recommended as the primary model due to its superior performance
- **SVM** remains a strong alternative with comparable results and faster training time
- **Ensemble approaches** can be considered for critical applications requiring maximum reliability
- All models are suitable for deployment given their high performance metrics
